# Jenkins.J._KFTA_05:  121

In the Summa theologiae, when Aquinas introduces the theological virtues, one of which is faith, he writes: Human beatitude or happiness ... is twofold.... One is proportionate to human nature, and this a human being can attain through the principles of his nature. The other is a beatitude surpassing the nature of a human being, which a person can attain only by divine power, through a certain participation in divinity.Read more at location 1762
