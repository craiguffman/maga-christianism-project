# a. The form of something refers to its function.  The soul is the form or function of the body.  Mind, the rational aspect of the soul (nous), draws conclusions from particular things.  But when the body is no longer receiving sense data, there is no function for the mind.  Therefore there is no sense in which the soul survives death.  He challenged the idea of eternal life.
