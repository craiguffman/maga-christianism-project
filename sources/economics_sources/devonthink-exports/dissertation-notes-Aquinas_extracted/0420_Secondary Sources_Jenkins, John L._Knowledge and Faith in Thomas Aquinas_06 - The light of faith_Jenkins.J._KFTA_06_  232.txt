# Jenkins.J._KFTA_06:  232

In the Scriptum super libros sententiarum, then, we found a strong though undeveloped suggestion that the process which issues in the assent of faith is analogous to the process of UNO-natural cognition which issues in judgments about principles. The latter process begins from sense perception, moves to the first operation of the intellect, the grasp of quiddities, and terminates with an act of the second operation of the intellect, the judgment of principles.
