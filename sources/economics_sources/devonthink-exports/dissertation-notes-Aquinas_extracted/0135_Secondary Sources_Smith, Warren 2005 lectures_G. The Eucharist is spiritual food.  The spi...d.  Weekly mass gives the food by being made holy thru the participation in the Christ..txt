# G. The Eucharist is spiritual food.  The spiritual life is analogous to the corporeal life.  In baptism we expeirence spiritual birth.  IN confirmation we expereince spiritual growth.  In Eucharist we are spiritually nourished.  Weekly mass gives the food by being made holy thru the participation in the Christ.
