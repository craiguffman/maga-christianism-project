# Jenkins.J._KFTA_05:  171

Curative grace heals those suffering the effects of sin, and sin is of two sorts: (I) the actual sins of an individual for which he alone is culpable; (2) the original sin committed by Adam and Eve for which all their descendants are in some way culpable.
