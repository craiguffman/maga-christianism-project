# Jenkins.J._KFTA_06:  227

I will argue that Aquinas's view of the warrant for faith is epistemically externalist and parallels his view of the assent to principles in merely human scientiae, discussed in chapter four above. Unlike the UNO-natural apprehension of principles, however, the cognitive faculties involved in the assent of faith are due to the supernatural gifts or graces from God.
