Aquinas's point is that these two processes are analogous, not identical. And he is saying that as the UNO-natural process begins with a certain sort of sense perception, so the process leading to faith begins with a certain sort of sense perception, namely, hearing a proclamation of the Creed, the articles of faith, presumably uttered and described as a report of God's word to the prospective believer.
