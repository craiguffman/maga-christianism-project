# Walsh.L._TheologyAquinas_Sacraments:  631

...one must recognize that whenever he talks about grace in those questions on sacraments, he is talking about the grace of the Holy Spirit; and when he is talking about the faith and charity and religion that come into play in sacraments, he understands those Spirit-formed virtues as accompanied by the corresponding gifts of the Holy Spirit; and when he is talking about the Church, it is the Church formed and animated by the Holy Spirit.  p. 331
