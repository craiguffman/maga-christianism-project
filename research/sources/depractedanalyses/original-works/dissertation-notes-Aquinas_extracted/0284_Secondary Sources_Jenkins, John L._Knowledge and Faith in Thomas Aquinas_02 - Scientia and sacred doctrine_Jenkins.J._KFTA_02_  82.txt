# Jenkins.J._KFTA_02:  82

Since God's revelation is received by a being of very limited intellectual powers, even when the human person is elevated by God's grace, this reception is in some respects limited and defective. In some respects, then, sacred doctrine is defective in relation to merely human scientiae and lacks some of the perfections they have.
