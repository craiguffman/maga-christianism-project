In Categories 12 and Metaphysics v. i i we find Aristotle's most lengthy discussions of priority and posteriority, and Aquinas commented only on the latter. In the Metaphysics commentary Aquinas explains: "For the principle in any genus is that which is first (primus) in the genus.
