# C. Why do we need a sign?  We need a sign - outward and visible - to discover the unknown thru the known.  Therefore GOd uses the commonplace - water, bread, wine - and uses them as points of contact by which to reveal God.   We are embodied creatures, therefore God uses the senses to inform the intellect.
