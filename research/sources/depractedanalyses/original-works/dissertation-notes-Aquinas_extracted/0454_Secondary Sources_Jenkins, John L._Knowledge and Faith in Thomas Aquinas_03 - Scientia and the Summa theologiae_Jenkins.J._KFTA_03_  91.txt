# Jenkins.J._KFTA_03:  91

Although God's own scientia is not discursive, human participation in it in sacred doctrine is, and in this scientia reasoning proceeds from the principles which have been divinely revealed to conclusions which can be drawn from them. In this discursive scientia the priority and doxastic causality conditions hold. And, as we will argue, this shapes Aquinas's project in the Summa theologiae. Read more at location 1092
