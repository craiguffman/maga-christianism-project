- **Primary Discipline**: Cultural Analysis/Sociology of Religion

## 2. Core Analysis
- **Central Thesis**: The displacement of religion from the center of American culture represents one of the most significant changes in American society, contributing to increased social hostility and division.
- **Key Arguments**: 
  - Religious intolerance was present from early American history
  - Modern America is experiencing a decline in communal spaces and reasons to congregate
  - The combination of growing wealth inequality and declining community is fueling hostility
  - Religious structures once provided social cohesion that is now missing
- **Methodology**: Historical observation combined with contemporary cultural analysis
- **Contextual Placement**: Contributes to discussions about secularization, social fragmentation, and the changing role of religion in public life
